## Reserved commercial feature

The `Multi-Server Cloud Service` feature is reserved for commercial licensing by the copyright owner.

The standard commercial price for this feature is `USD 1.00 per CPU core per month`, billed by provisioned virtual unit, virtual private core, or equivalent compute core allocation (`PVU/VPC`), unless the copyright owner agrees to different written terms.

For this policy, `Multi-Server Cloud Service` means any software, module, service, connector, or control-plane capability that is designated by the project maintainer as supporting one or more of the following:

- centralized management of multiple servers or nodes from one panel
- clustered or federated control planes
- remote provisioning or orchestration across multiple servers
- hosted or managed control-plane service operations
- tenant management across multiple physical or virtual servers
- related enterprise add-ons, connectors, packaging, or documentation delivered as part of that feature set

Unless the copyright owner expressly releases a specific implementation of that feature under AGPL in writing, all rights in that reserved feature remain proprietary and are withheld from the AGPL grant.
